Episodes

Season 1

1. Secrets

When a young boy goes missing, a local family is torn apart. Strange events plague the small town of Winden as dark secrets resurface.

2. Lies

The investigation deepens as connections between families are revealed. A strange device is discovered near the caves, hinting at hidden experiments.

3. Past and Present

Strange occurrences continue as time seems to bend around Winden. New disappearances mirror incidents from the past, suggesting a pattern.

4. The Travelers

A mysterious stranger appears in town with knowledge of future events. The existence of time travel is slowly uncovered through hidden diary entries.

5. Ghosts

The boundaries between past and present blur as characters encounter versions of themselves and others from different times.

6. A Kind of Doom

The full extent of the conspiracy is revealed. A time machine hidden in the caves serves a sinister purpose tied to an apocalypse.

7. Crossroads

Key characters make choices that set events in motion across multiple time periods. The loop begins to close on itself.

8. As You Know

The season finale reveals the shocking truth about Jonas and his role as 'The Traveler'. The apocalypse approaches as timelines collapse.

9. The End

Dark matter and temporal experiments culminate in a catastrophic event. The final episode leaves viewers with shocking revelations about time itself.

10. Uncertainty Principle

The season concludes with a massive cliffhanger introducing parallel worlds. Nothing is as it seems in the fabric of reality.

Season 2

1. Beginnings

A new timeline emerges with parallel versions of Winden. Characters discover the existence of another world where different choices were made.

2. Dark Matter

Dark matter experiments reveal the nature of the time loop. Both worlds share identical family structures but diverging destinies.

3. The Travelers

Multiple travelers from different times converge on Winden. The conflict between worlds intensifies as versions of themselves meet.

4. The Defiant Ones

Characters attempt to break the cycle by making different choices. But every action seems predetermined in the loop.

5. Lost and Found

Families are torn apart as members from both worlds collide. Emotional reunions and heartbreaking revelations define this turning point.

6. An Endless Cycle

The 33-year cycles are explained, showing how the loop perpetually repeats. All events are connected across time.

7. Between the Lines

Hidden connections between world versions emerge through diary entries and secret meetings. The truth lies between what is known and hidden.

8. Endings and Beginnings

The season finale reveals the origin of the time machine and its creator. Reality shatters with revelations about the first time cycle.

Season 3

1. Deja-vu

The apocalypse arrives as predicted. Reality fractures across infinite worlds, threatening to collapse entirely.

2. The Survivors

Survivors from both worlds band together to find a way to break the cycle. Time travel becomes their only weapon against fate.

3. The Origin

The bootstrap paradox is explored as characters discover who truly created the time machine. Origin and end blur into one.

4. The Participants

All players in the time loop are revealed. Their interconnected fates span centuries across two worlds in constant struggle.

5. The Knot

The metaphorical 'knot' binding all timelines together begins to unravel. A solution emerges requiring the ultimate sacrifice.

6. Light and Shadow

Good and evil, multiple worlds and infinite possibilities converge. The final confrontation between worlds determines reality itself.

7. The White Knot

Characters execute their plan to break free from the infinite loop. Sacrifice and redemption define the penultimate chapter.

8. The End of the Beginning

The series finale. Reality stabilizes as timelines collapse into one. Existence itself is rewritten as characters achieve their freedom.
