Timeline of Dark

Origin World

Everything begins in a single origin world where a catastrophic accident at a nuclear power plant sets off a chain of events. This world contains the initial source of all temporal manipulation and the creation of the time machine. The experiments conducted here breach the barriers between time itself, forever altering the fabric of reality.

Creation of the Time Loop

The God Particle and chair-based time machine are developed, enabling travel through time. However, this innovation creates a bootstrap paradox where the creator and creation become cyclically dependent. Each action taken to prevent the loop only strengthens it further, creating a perfect closed circle of causality.

Multiple Time Periods

The loop spans multiple generations across 33-year cycles. Key events repeat: children disappear, families are torn apart, and secrets resurface. Characters from different time periods interact and influence one another. The timeline includes the 1890s, 1920s, 1950s, 1980s, and 2020s, each with its own version of Winden and its inhabitants.

The Two Worlds

A parallel world is discovered where events diverged at a critical moment. Both worlds contain the same families but with different choices and outcomes. They are quantum entangled, bound together by the same time loop. The existence of both worlds creates a knot of causality that seems unbreakable, with inhabitants unaware of their counterparts.

The Apocalypse

The loop is designed to end in apocalypse. Both worlds face simultaneous destruction as their timelines collapse. The God Particle becomes unstable, reality fractures, and time itself ceases to function. This moment is inevitable within the loop, repeated endlessly until the cycle is broken.

Breaking the Loop

The final stage involves characters discovering the origin point of the loop and making the choice to end it. By preventing the creation of the time machine or undoing the original accident, the loop can be broken. However, this requires sacrifice and means erasing worlds and the people within them from existence.
